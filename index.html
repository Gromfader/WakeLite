We at Wake Lite seek to increase

Are you tired of being tired?

We know the struggle of
getting up in the morning.

We know that our wake up
routines needs to change.

We know the answer!

The Wake Lite Bulb will slowly brighten up in the morning to simulate genuine sunlight and prevents sudden wake-ups that disrupt the natural sleep cycle.

By analyzing sleeping patterns, our product will detect when your deep sleep is nearing an end and slowly light up as you get closer to the lighter sleep phase.

This ensures that you never wake up in the middle of deep sleep as it will increasingly wake you when your body is naturally ready.
